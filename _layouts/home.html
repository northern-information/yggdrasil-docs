Setup

Abstract

Yggsdrasil is a livecoding tracker for norns. Yggsdrasil is designed to be performed with a keyboard only. Capital letters are not welcome here and the shift keys are used sparingly. The ; is under the little finger: economic, ergonomic, exegesis.

Commands and interactions are the two core concepts of Yggdrasil.

"Commands" are built from the letters, numbers, and symbols that appear in the terminal as you type. Typically, commands will do things like change notes, control playback, or adjust tempo. Yet other commands will let you copy entire columns, route notes to different MIDI channels, or even create musical patterns.

"Interactions" are simply everything else. While some interactions will manipulate sound (such as backspace), most are simply affordances for navigatation — things like toggling views, dismissing messages, or paging through through command history.
